Privacy policy

The Company recognizes protection of the interests and personal information of individuals as an important responsibility and will adhere to the policy set out below when acquiring, using, managing and otherwise handling personal information in connection with its business operations.

  1. The Company will handle personal information properly in compliance with applicable laws, regulations and guidelines and in compliance with its own internal rules setting out the handling of internal rules for the protection of personal information.
  2. The Company will only acquire personal information by legitimate means.
  3. The Company will specify the purpose of use of personal information to the extent possible and will promptly inform you of the purpose of use, except in cases where the purpose of use is announced or publicized in advance. The Company will use your personal information only to the extent necessary to achieve the purpose of use.
  4. The Company will obtain your consent before disclosing your personal data to any third party or will ensure that that necessary details of such disclosure are readily accessible to you. If the Company shares your personal data, it will inform you who it is sharing your personal data with and for what purpose or will ensure that that necessary details of such disclosure are readily accessible to you. If the Company entrusts a third party to handle your personal data, it will take appropriate steps to ensure that your personal data is managed securely.
  5. The Company will provide education and training to its officers, employees and others to raise awareness of its privacy policy and internal rules.
  6. The Company will establish organizations and a framework of responsibility to properly manage personal data and will also audit implementation status in a timely manner and make improvements without delay where necessary. The Company will also introduce appropriate security measures to prevent the leak or loss of personal data and destroy or delete, without delay, any personal data which it no longer needs to use. In the event of a leak or other information incident, the Company will promptly take steps to rectify the situation.
  7. The Company will endeavor to ensure the accuracy of personal data and with establish a point of contact for dealing appropriately with complaints related to personal information or requests for disclosure, correction, or cessation of use of your personal data.

The Company will review this policy as appropriate and seek to improve it on an ongoing basis.
