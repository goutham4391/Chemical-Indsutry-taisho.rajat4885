Sports Sponsorship

Taisho Pharmaceutical is an enthusiastic supporter of sports culture. We believe that the pursuit of sports leads to the cultivation of a sound mind in a sound body.

Taisho Pharmaceutical believes that supporting sports culture will lead to increasing the number of people who participate in competitive sports, as well as their ranks of supporters. In turn, such efforts will support the development of competitive sports, and by extension the fulfillment of the Company's mission.

At Taisho Pharmaceutical, our conviction is that committing Company earnings to furnishing a forward-looking environment for consumers to enjoy is an ideal state of affairs for a pharmaceutical company. We are passionate about supporting the development of sports culture over the medium-to-long term.

Rugby Football

Since 2001, Taisho Pharmaceutical has supported the Japan Rugby Football Union team. We launched the Lipovitan D Challenge Cup in 2002, serving continuously as this event's main sponsor.

We also served as official sponsor of the Rugby World Cup 2019â„¢ Japan tournament, which marked the first time the competition was staged in Japan. As a top partner of the Japan Rugby Football Union team, Taisho Pharmaceutical will continue to support the development of rugby in Japan.

Taisho Pharmaceutical also organizes rugby classes for children. We invite former members of the Japan Rugby Football Union team as well as active and former players in the Japan Rugby Top League to take part as instructors.
Our hope is that children will grow to love this sport, thereby supporting the future development of rugby.


Baseball

Taisho Pharmaceutical has served as a special sponsor of the Nippon Professional Baseball (NPB) Amateur Player Drafts since 2013. The Company has also been the main sponsor of the NPB Awards since 2014 and an NPB partner since 2016.
Taisho Pharmaceutical is also a proud supporter of amateur sports. As a supporter of the activities of the World Children's Baseball Foundation, proposed and launched by Sadaharu Oh and the late Hank Aaron, we sponsor the World Children's Baseball Fair and baseball classes. We also provide assistance in organizing events mainly for boys held by the Tokyo Nanshiki Baseball Association and the Setagaya Baseball League. We support baseball in different categories, including the Boys League, the Little Senior League, the Tokyo Big-6 Baseball League and the Intercity Baseball Tournament.


Tennis

In 2020, Taisho Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. signed an official sponsorship agreement with the Japan Tennis Association. We support players' continuing efforts to achieve their dreams through the various tournaments, schools and events organized by the association.
At the same time, we began support for Kimiko Date x Yonex Project: Go for the Grand Slam, a program to train top junior female players in the six to eleventh grades. With this activity, we will provide support for children who dream of playing in Junior Grand Slams with a view to competing as professionals in the global arena.
