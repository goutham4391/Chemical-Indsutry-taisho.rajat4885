Web Accessibility

Taisho Pharmaceutical Holdings Co., Ltd. (hereinafter the “Company”) strives to improve the accessibility of its Website so that the information and functions provided can be accessed and used by anyone irrespective of physical or mental condition and the environment used.

We have also introduced FACIL’iti, a tool that enables a more comfortable browsing experience for people with special needs such as visual, motor and cognitive disorders.

FACIL’iti

FACIL’iti is a tool that provides a more comfortable browsing experience according to a user’s visual, motor and cognitive disorders. The ease of viewing a website differs depending on age, gender, physical characteristics and circumstances. With FACIL’iti, it is possible to optimize the website display in line with the needs of the user with just a few clicks.

The appearance of the Company’s Website can be customized to fit needs accompanying various conditions such as low vision, Parkinson’s disease and dyslexia simply by entering one’s personal settings on FACIL’iti (Please note that certain content and PDF files may not change).

Supported conditions

Vision: Cataracts, age-related macular degeneration, presbyopia, color blindness, etc.
Movement: Essential tremor, Parkinson’s disease, etc.
Cognitive: Dyslexia, photosensitive epilepsy, etc.

How to use FACIL’iti

  1. Open the FACIL’iti settings page and access personal settings.
    Click here to open the FACIL’iti settings page
  2. Register your preferred settings based on a preset list, with options including Parkinson’s disease, cataracts or presbyopia, or apply customized settings to fit your condition.
  3. Once you return to the Company’s Website, it will be adjusted to your requirements. Your personal settings will also be applied to all other websites that have installed FACIL’iti.
